What is Betty?
What we are
An online casino — slots, bingo, and live casino games
Where we operate
Ontario & Alberta, Canada — licensed by AGCO. Players must be physically present in the province they registered in to play.
Most important rule
Players must be physically located in Ontario or Alberta to play — being in another province (e.g. Quebec, BC) means they cannot access games
Common mix-up
betty.ca is our site. betty.com is a completely separate, unrelated company. Players often confuse the two.
Key terms you may hear
  • Betty coins — in-app currency players earn through gameplay, spendable in the Betty store. They have no cash value and you cannot give them out as compensation.
  • Crack the Code — an in-game bingo feature where players can win free bingo tickets. These tickets are only valid for the very next bingo round — they expire if not used immediately and are not replaced.
  • Cashback promotion — an automated weekly rebate on net losses. Only triggers if net losses exceed $100 for the week. Shows as $0 if the threshold hasn't been reached.
  • VIP host — a dedicated account manager for high-value players. VIP hosts look after bonusing their players, so bonus requests should always be escalated to them rather than handled directly by CS.
  • Promotions / promos — deposit bonuses or special offers with variable eligibility criteria, including a minimum deposit requirement or minimum play on a specific game. Sometimes refers to Betty loyalty and rewards programs rather than specific campaigns. Promos are tailored per player.
What you can and cannot do
  • ✓ You can: explain policies clearly, guide players through withdrawals, acknowledge frustration, escalate to a VIP host or manager, pass feedback to the team
  • ✗ You cannot: reinstate expired tickets or bonuses, offer Betty coins or cash as compensation, approve bonus requests, override game outcomes
  • If a player pushes back, stay warm but hold the policy line. A clear, empathetic "no" is better than a vague maybe.
What CS Agents deal with every day
  • Players from other provinces who deposited but can't play — need a clear, kind explanation and help withdrawing funds
  • Bonus and promo confusion — why is my cashback $0? Why don't I qualify for this promo?
  • Promotion or reward disputes — a player won a promotional reward (e.g. free tickets, bonus spins) but it expired or disappeared before they could use it
  • Frustrated players who want compensation or feel treated unfairly — de-escalate, don't fix everything
